Why StreamX Exists

On platforms like YouTube, TikTok, and Facebook, millions of talented creators struggle every day — not because their content isn't good enough, but because the system isn't built in their favour. Discoverability is broken. Competition is brutal. And earnings? Often unfair.

StreamX was built from the ground up to fix that. A platform where a new creator gets a genuine chance, where the algorithm works for the creator — not against them — and where the revenue share is actually honest.

Launched on April 20, 2026, StreamX is a global platform open to creators and viewers everywhere in the world.
