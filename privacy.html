🔒 Your privacy matters to us. StreamX is committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about what data we collect and why. By using StreamX, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Phone number (used for OTP-based authentication)
  • Username and display name
  • Profile photo and bio
  • Videos, comments, and other content you upload or post
  • Messages sent through the platform

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages viewed, videos watched, and time spent
  • Search queries and interactions on StreamX
  • App performance data and crash reports

Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication data from Firebase (Google) for phone OTP verification
  • Analytics data from integrated analytics services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve StreamX services
  • Authenticate your identity and keep your account secure
  • Personalize your feed and recommendations using AI
  • Enable live streaming, live shopping, and social commerce features
  • Send you important notifications about your account
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and policy violations
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Display relevant advertisements (if applicable)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in the following cases:

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party providers (such as Firebase/Google for authentication, cloud hosting providers) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

Public Content

Videos, comments, and profile information you make public on StreamX are visible to all users and may be indexed by search engines.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required by Pakistani law, court orders, or government authorities.

Business Transfers

If StreamX is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Cookies & Tracking

StreamX uses cookies and similar technologies (including IndexedDB for offline storage) to:

  • Keep you logged in between sessions
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Enable offline functionality via PWA service worker
  • Measure performance and usage analytics
  • Serve relevant advertisements (if AdSense is enabled)

You can control cookie settings in your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some features.

We use Google AdSense which may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

Offline data (videos cached for offline viewing) is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB and is only accessible to you.

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Download your content and data
  • Restrict certain types of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@streamx.pk

7. Children's Privacy

StreamX is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on StreamX or sending you a notification. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@streamx.pk
  • Website: streamx.pk/contact

We will respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
