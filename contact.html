Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a creator on StreamX? +
Anyone can become a creator on StreamX! Simply sign up with your phone number, complete your profile, and start uploading. Creator monetization features unlock once you meet our community milestones.
How does StreamX's Trust Score work? +
Trust Score is StreamX's community health system. It tracks account behaviour, content quality, and community engagement. Higher Trust Scores unlock more platform features and visibility.
Can I use StreamX offline? +
Yes! StreamX is a Progressive Web App (PWA) with offline support. Save videos while connected and watch them later without internet. The app also works smoothly on slow connections.
How do I report content or a user? +
You can report any video or user directly from the platform using the "..." menu. Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes action within 24-48 hours.
Is StreamX available on Android? +
StreamX is available as a PWA that works on all Android browsers. A dedicated Android app (TWA) is coming to the Google Play Store soon!
