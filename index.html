Privacy Policy

Stream x Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, profile picture, and content you upload. We also collect usage data including videos watched, liked, and shared.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide personalized content recommendations, improve our algorithm, send notifications about activity on your content, and maintain your account security.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). Media files are stored on Cloudinary servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can edit your profile information, delete your uploaded content, and control your notification preferences.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your theme preference, watch history, and search history. This data stays on your device and is not shared.

6. Content Policy

Users are responsible for content they upload. Stream x reserves the right to remove content that violates our community guidelines including spam, harassment, or illegal content.

7. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us through the Stream x platform. We respond within 48 hours.

By using Stream x, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy and will notify users of significant changes.

Content Policy

Stream x is committed to keeping the platform safe and positive.

Community Guidelines

✅ Allowed Content

• Original videos, shorts, images and posts
• Educational and informational content
• Entertainment, comedy, gaming, music
• News and current affairs (factual)
• Sports highlights (with permission)
• Islamic content, cultural content

❌ Prohibited Content

• Nudity or sexually explicit content
• Violence, gore or graphic content
• Hate speech or discrimination
• Harassment or bullying
• Spam or misleading content
• Illegal activities or dangerous acts
• Content involving minors inappropriately
• Copyright infringing material

🚩 Reporting Violations

See a violation? Use the 3-dot menu on any video or post and tap Report. Our team reviews all reports within 24-48 hours.

⚠️ Consequences

• First violation: Content removed + warning
• Second violation: 7-day upload restriction
• Third violation: Permanent account suspension
• Illegal content: Immediate removal + report to authorities

Stream x reserves the right to remove any content or account at its discretion to maintain a safe platform for all users.

Copyright Policy

Stream x Copyright Policy

Stream x respects intellectual property rights and expects users to do the same.

What is Copyright Infringement?

Uploading content that you do not own or have permission to use — including music, videos, images, or other creative works — is copyright infringement.

DMCA Policy

Stream x follows DMCA (Digital Millennium Copyright Act) guidelines. Copyright holders can report infringing content and we will review and act accordingly.

What Happens to Violators?

• First violation: Warning issued
• Second violation: Content removed, channel restricted
• Third violation: Account permanently suspended

Fair Use

Commentary, criticism, education, and news reporting may qualify as fair use. However, this does not protect all uses of copyrighted material.

Original Content

Always upload content you created yourself. Use royalty-free music and images from approved sources when creating videos.

Report Copyright Violation

Use the Report button on any video to report copyright violations. Our team reviews all reports within 72 hours.

Protect your original content by uploading it on Stream x. Your content is your property.
